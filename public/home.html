QuickBase Voice Messages

Send the text in your QuickBase records as voice messages.

This site reads text from a QuickBase record and plays it as an audio voice message to a phone number.

You will need an account on Twilio and access to QuickBase.

From your Twillio account, you will need the 'Account SID', 'Auth Token' and the verified phone number that Twilio should use for outbound messages.

Use this form to test whether you have the right information to send voice messages.

Use this form to send bulk voice messages using a 'Pending Voice Messages' report in QuickBase.

The source code for this site is at https://github.com/garethlatwork/quickbase-voice-messages.
